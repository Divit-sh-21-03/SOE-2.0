About ElectroMos

ElectroMos is the premier electronics club of IIT Bhilai, dedicated to fostering innovation and hands-on learning in electronics and embedded systems. We organize workshops, hackathons, and technical events to help students build practical skills and explore cutting-edge technologies. Our mission is to create a vibrant community of electronics enthusiasts who are passionate about building the future through technology and innovation.
